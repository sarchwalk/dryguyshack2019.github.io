Driveway and Pathway Roomba

Dry Guys 2019

The landscaping roomba is a modified roomba that takes in weather data.
It shovels and salts the ground.
It launches whenever snow is falling.

Benefits

Shoveling requires a lot of energy and time to do, or expensive machinery.
The landscaping roomba requires no supervision.
It won't require large outputs of energy as it will be able to work semi-continuously.
No toxic fumes as it uses an electric motor.
It will also be rather inexpensive, and quiet compares to snowblowers.

How?

Continuous work prevents snow accumulation and ice formation.
Docking stations will charge the roomba, and refill the salt.
Employing a data scraper will allow the roomba to only work when it snows based on up-to-date information.

Marketability

Ideal for universities, parks, and other areas that have walkways that need to be cleared of snow.
Can help push cities, like Calgary, as bike-friendly by clearing bike-paths.

Security

Docking station and roombas will have IDs that will need to be verified to work.
