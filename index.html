SNOOMBA: The Roomba for Driveway and Pathway Clearing

Dry Guys 2019

The SNOOMBA is a modified roomba that clears and salts snow covered driveways and pathways.
SNOOMBA employs the use of artificial intelligence to determine when weather is appropriate to start work.

How?

Shoveling requires a lot of energy and time to do, or expensive machinery.
The landscaping roomba requires no supervision.
It won't require large outputs of energy as it will be able to work semi-continuously.
No toxic fumes as it uses an electric motor.
It will also be rather inexpensive, and quiet compared to snowblowers.

Marketability

Ideal for universities, parks, homes, and other areas that have walkways that need to be cleared of snow.
Can help push cities, like Calgary, as bike-friendly by clearing bike-paths.

Security

Docking station and roombas will have IDs that will need to be verified to work.
Integrated GPS allows easy tracking of roombas if they get misplaced.
Visible paint and flag will reduce accidents involving the roombas.

The SNOOMBA Continuous work prevents snow accumulation and ice formation.
Docking stations will charge the roomba, and refill the salt.
Employing a data scraper will allow the roomba to only work when it snows based on up-to-date information.
Larger wheels make the roomba be able to traverse more difficult terrain.
